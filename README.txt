VIRAL ANIME WATER REMINDER — ANDROID

WHAT IT DOES
- Floating anime character over other apps.
- Hourly water reminder.
- Done button.
- Snooze 10 minutes.
- Restarts reminders after phone reboot.
- Test button for immediate preview.

BUILD
1. Open this folder in Android Studio.
2. Allow Gradle sync.
3. Connect your Android phone with USB debugging enabled, then press Run.
   Or use Build > Build APK(s).
4. On the phone, open the app.
5. Tap "Allow floating character" and enable Display over other apps.
6. Return to app and tap "Start hourly reminders".
7. Tap "Test floating reminder now" to verify immediately.

NOTE
Android/OEM battery optimization can delay background alarms on some devices.
